My Story

Hello! I'm Ewuradjoa Badji, a passionate media producer with over 8 years of experience in visual storytelling. My journey began in film school, where I discovered the power of combining technical skill with emotional narrative.

I believe every project is an opportunity to create something meaningful — whether it's a 30-second commercial or a feature-length documentary. My approach combines meticulous planning with spontaneous creativity, allowing for both structure and magic in the production process.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working with diverse clients and teams, from independent artists to global corporations. Each collaboration has taught me that the best media production happens at the intersection of vision, technology, and human connection.

When I'm not on set or in the editing suite, I'm exploring new storytelling technologies, mentoring emerging filmmakers, and seeking inspiration in the world around me.
